Rhythm Frenzy Rules

Rhythm Frenzy is a Rhythm game where the player needs to press keys at the right timing to gain points

  • Use the S D J and K keys for each column of the game.
  • Press them at the right timing to gain points.
  • Pressing it without rectangles to validate or missing them will take points away.
  • The game last for 30s, good luck !

Scoring

  • Each correct key press will give you points.
  • The closest you are to the middle, the more you'll get points.
  • Missing a rectangle will take away 5 points.
  • Pressing a key at a wrong time will take away 5 points.
